                          错题集时态语态

                          wuxuefeng大约 2 分钟笔记English
                          此页内容

                          #
                          I will tell hime as soon as he ____ back
                          B
                          A

                          A:come
                          B:comes

                          C:will come
                          D:came
                          主将从现,不要忘了主谓一致
                          as soon as 一....就....

                          #
                          The picnic will be put off if it ____ tomorrow
                          B
                          C

                          A:will snow
                          B:snows

                          C:is snowing
                          D:snowed
                          主将从现,不需要be doing来表将来
                          1 put off 拖迟

                          #
                          David is so excited because it is the first time that he ___ the Great Wall
                          C
                          A

                          A:visited
                          B:had visited

                          C: has visited
                          D:visits
                          现在完成时,这是我第一次做...事
                          1 the first time 第一次做...事

                          #
                          Tom had to pack his things up quickly,because he was told that his taxi ___ in 2 minutes
                          A
                          B

                          A:was leaving
                          B:will leave

                          C:left
                          D:is leaving
                          told表示是过去说的,车将要在两分钟后离开,也就是过去将来时态
                          1 things up 东西

                          #
                          A new stadium ___ at present in our city
                          C
                          A

                          A:is built
                          B:is being build

                          C:is being built
                          D:is building
                          正在被修建,现在进行时态被动
                          1 stadium 体育馆

                          #
                          --Nancy has worked in our company for three years,She speaks French very fluently.--She ___ French for four years in university
                          A
                          B

                          A:studied
                          B:had studied

                          C:has studied
                          D:study
                          这里只是叙述他过去学了四年法语,没有对后面的时间产生影响,因此用过去时态
                          1 无

                          #
                          The plane ___ at 8:30 am,so I ___ home at 7:00 tomorrow morning
                          D
                          B

                          A:leaves;leave
                          B:is leaving;am leaving

                          C:is leaving;leave
                          D:leaves;am leaving
                          第一个空,leave位移动词如果是按照明确的时间表规定可以用现一般现在表将来,第二个be doing表将来
                          1 无

                          #
                          They fulfilled the plan earlier than they ___
                          D
                          B

                          A:have expected
                          B:expected

                          C:were expected
                          D:had expected
                          他们期待的是一个时间点,比他们期待的还要早,过去的过去,过去完成时态
                          1 fulfill 完成

                          #
                          Up to now,he ___ chairman of the Students' Union twice in our school
                          D
                          A

                          A:has selected
                          B:elects

                          C:is selected
                          D:has been selected
                          现在完成时被动,被选举成主席
                          1 up to now 到目前为止

                          #
                          The boy ___ to take care of his bike,because it needs ___
                          D
                          C

                          A:was told;to repair
                          B:told;to be repaired

                          C:told;repairing
                          D:is told;repairing
                          这个男孩被告知要照顾好他的自行车,need主动表示被动
                          1 take care 当心

                          #
                          A brief History of Mathematics ___ in 1988 and has been translated into more than 40 languages ever since
                          A
                          D

                          A:was published
                          B:were published

                          C:have been published
                          D:has been published
                          被发布,in 1988明确的过去时间,过去被动
                          1 publish 发布
                          2 ever since 至今

                          #
                          He said that great changes ___ in his hometown since 1990
                          C
                          D

                          A:have taken place
                          B:have been taken place

                          C:had taken place
                          D:had been taken place
                          自从1990发生,过去完成!!!发生没有被动
                          1 since 自从
